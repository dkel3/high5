Standing Ovation Award

Use this award to recognize individuals for exceptional actions that have made an impact in moving or sustaining the Pillars of Performance at the team, department, organization, or enterprise level. Standing Ovation Awards involve a nomination process and require officer approval. Standing Ovation Awards are Allstate Technology & Operations (ATOs) top honor.
These are the levels and values associated with the Standing Ovation award (Please note values differ between Business Areas *).

Level 1 - Exceptional performance that impacts a team or department project/goal. - 100 points; Points count toward eligibility for year-end rewards. They cannot be used to redeem items out of the catalog.

Level 2 - Exceptional performance that impacts an ATO or enterprise-wide project/goal.- 200 points; Points count toward eligibility for year-end rewards. They cannot be used to redeem items out of the catalog.

Standing Ovation

To nominate an employee for a Standing Ovation Award: Select the employee by entering their name or NTID, a Level and one or more Reasons ('Ctrl' key + select for multiples): (if name does not appear, refer to First and Last Name areas in the Outlook Address Book)
