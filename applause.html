Applause Award

Use this award to recognize individuals for performing actions that go above and beyond normal work expectations, goal accomplishments, and/or exemplifying Organizational Success Factors (OSFs). Applause Awards are linked to point values of 15 points and have monetary and non-monetary options.

Applause

To nominate an employee for a Applause Award: Select the employee by entering their name or NTID, a Level and one or more Reasons ('Ctrl' key + select for multiples): (if name does not appear, refer to First and Last Name areas in the Outlook Address Book)
