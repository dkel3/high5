Welcome to High5!

High 5 is Allstate Technology & Operations’ (ATO’s) online recognition program. Aligned to the principles and values in our ATO Pillars of Performance and Organizational Success Factors (OSFs), High 5 brings a comprehensive and consistent approach to recognition in ATO, including Allstate Northern Ireland (ANI), as well as extends parts of the program to our partners in Allstate Financial Technology (AFT) and Allstate Investments Technology.

High 5 takes a centralized approach, allowing us to create the best environment for recognition in ATO by anchoring recognition to our goals, objectives and desired behaviors; documenting and encouraging employees to use recognition; and hold leaders accountable to fostering an environment of recognition.
